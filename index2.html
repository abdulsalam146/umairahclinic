About Dr. Umairah Yaqub ڈاکٹر امیرہ یعقوب کے بارے میں

Dedicated Women's Healthcare Specialist

خواتین کی صحت کی ماہر

With 18 years of clinical, academic, surgical, and administrative experience in Obstetrics & Gynecology at Pakistan Armed Forces tertiary care hospitals, I bring a depth of expertise to every patient interaction—including 13 years in senior consultant positions.

My practice is built on extensive involvement in undergraduate and postgraduate medical education, CPSP supervision and examination, advanced gynecologic surgery, maternal medicine, and gynecologic oncology.

International Exposure

  • RCOG-funded maternal medicine placement in the UK.
  • Service as Consultant Gynecologist with the United Nations in Sudan (UNAMID).

Comprehensive Women's Health Services

C-Section & Surgery

Gynecological Problems
